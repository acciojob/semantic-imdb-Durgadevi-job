Summary

Inception is a movie about a skilled thief named Dom Cobb who enters people's dreams to steal secrets. He is offered a chance to erase his criminal past by planting an idea into a person's subconscious mind.

Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Tom Hardy, and Elliot Page played major roles in the movie.
